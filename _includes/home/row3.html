Living life fully on the little red dot

At the age of twenty, I decide to set off into the world to explore the possibilities. My journey starts from being a junior show presentor in the zoo. Subsequently, I am being introduced into the world of dance, fitness and yoga. After the past years of exploration, I have found pieces of me. Things got better, I step into the world of children and teenage training. Then, I got involved in technology and events.

Over and over, life has provided me with ample opportunities to fall in love with myself and life. Right now, I hope to optimally utilise my experience and skills of teaching, training, planning and web development to help people explore their potential.

In a nutshell, my mission in life?

To positively impact and develop our next generation.
